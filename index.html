What I Offer

Services tailored for Brazilian audiences.

From in-app copy to marketing campaigns — every word is reviewed by a native PT-BR expert with deep experience across technology, streaming, and software.

01
Localization QA

Thorough review of localized content for linguistic accuracy, cultural fit, consistency, and tone — ensuring your PT-BR release is flawless before it reaches users.

02
Software UI Testing

Functional and linguistic testing of software interfaces in Brazilian Portuguese — catching text expansion issues, broken strings, and mistranslations before launch.

03
Copy Editing

Expert proofreading and editing of Portuguese copy — from website content to product descriptions, ensuring natural, compelling, and grammatically precise language.

04
Marketing Localization

Culturally adapted marketing materials that resonate with the Brazilian market — from campaign slogans and email sequences to social content and product storytelling.

Media & Streaming QA
